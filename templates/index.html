Why Tuber?

Tuber significantly reduces the human resource and expertise required for diagnosing tuberculosis and malaria and makes the entire system far more efficient and less prone to error. Tuberculosis and Malaria would have a huge impact in rural areas in South Asian and African nations, where there is acute lack of qualified doctors to diagnose and treat such diseases.
